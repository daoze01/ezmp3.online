FAQ

Is ezmp3 completely free?

Yes, ezmp3 is completely free to use, with no hidden fees. We support our service through minimal ads.

What video platforms are supported?

We support YouTube, Vimeo, Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, and many other popular video platforms.

What is the maximum file size I can convert?

Our service can handle videos up to 12 hours long. For longer videos, we recommend splitting them into smaller segments.

How long does conversion take?

Most videos complete conversion in 30-60 seconds, depending on video length and our server load.

What audio quality options are available?

We offer multiple audio quality options: 64kbps (minimum file size), 128kbps (recommended), 192kbps (high quality), and 320kbps (highest quality). You can choose the quality you prefer before conversion.

Can I use ezmp3 on mobile devices?

Yes, ezmp3 runs perfectly on all mobile devices, including iPhones, iPads, and Android devices.

Where are downloaded files saved?

Files are saved to your default download location in your browser. You can view download history and find all MP3 files by clicking the "Download History" button in the top menu.

Is converting YouTube videos to MP3 legal?

In many jurisdictions, personal video conversion is considered fair use. However, distributing copyrighted material is illegal. Please respect copyright law and only convert videos you are authorized to use.

How to report issues or provide feedback?

If you encounter any issues or want to provide feedback, please use the contact form at the bottom of the page or send an email to support@ezmp3.cc.
